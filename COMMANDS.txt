GENERAL:
ds - display stats
dbs - display boss stats 
lb - list inventory
ab - inventory menu, access things from inventory.

OPERATORS:
+ - combine commands/choices/moves together
@ - from function 

MOVEMENT:
r - right
l - left
g - grab an item


COMBAT:
dg - dodge 
t - teleport
ua - upper attack
da - downwards attack
fa - forward attack
